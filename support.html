Frequently Asked Questions

How do I connect a new device to the network?

expand_more
To connect a new device, log in to your account dashboard and navigate to the 'My Devices' section. Click 'Add Device' and enter the MAC address found in your device settings. Your device should be whitelisted within 2 minutes.

Why is my connection speed slow?

expand_more
Speed issues can occur during peak usage hours (7PM - 10PM). First, try restarting your device. If the issue persists, check if you are downloading large files, which might be throttled according to our Fair Use Policy. You can run a speed test from the Dashboard.

How do I request a refund?

expand_more
Refunds are processed for service outages lasting longer than 4 hours. To request a refund, go to the 'Billing' tab and select the transaction in question. Click 'Report Issue' and select 'Refund Request'. Our team reviews these within 24 hours.

What do I do if I forgot my password?

expand_more
Click the 'Login' button at the top right, then select 'Forgot Password'. Enter the email address associated with your account, and we will send you a secure link to reset your credentials.

Can I share my account with others?

expand_more
Each account is limited to a specific number of simultaneous devices based on your plan (usually 1 or 3). Sharing your login credentials is contrary to our Terms of Service and may result in account suspension.

How do I upgrade my plan?

expand_more
Visit your Dashboard and click the 'Upgrade Plan' button on your current subscription card. Select your new plan and complete the payment. The upgrade takes effect immediately, and you'll be charged the prorated difference.

What payment methods do you accept?

expand_more
We accept M-Pesa (via STK Push), Visa, Mastercard, and PayPal. All payments are processed securely through encrypted channels. For monthly subscriptions, we support automatic recurring billing.

Can't find what you're looking for?

Our support team is available 24/7 to help you get back online. Submit a ticket and we'll respond shortly.
